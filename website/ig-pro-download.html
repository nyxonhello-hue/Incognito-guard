🚀 Quick Setup Guide

1
Extract the zip to your Desktop or any folder.
2
Open config.json in Notepad (Windows) or any text editor. Fill in your email address, Gmail App Password, child's name, and device name.
3
Run the installer:
Windows: Right-click install.ps1 → Run with PowerShell as Administrator
Linux/macOS: Open terminal → sudo bash install.sh YOUR_EXTENSION_ID
4
Load the browser extension: Open chrome://extensions → Developer Mode → Load Unpacked → select the extension/chromium folder → click Details → turn on Allow in Incognito.
5
Launch IncognitoGuard-Pro and open Settings to verify your email. Test by opening an incognito tab — it should close instantly and you should receive an email alert.
Need help with setup? Contact us at incognito-guard.vercel.app/ig-contact.html — we'll walk you through it.
